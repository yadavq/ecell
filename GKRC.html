GIIEC Kerala Releif Campaign

Date: 29-30 August 2018

Filling waters in all sideways and byways

Leaking through all alleys and subways

Overflowing canals, rivers and bays

Overflowing streets, stations and houses

Driving people into despair and madness...

Flood in Kerala had caused great loss of lives, wealth. Many people had lost their lives. Blood has been harder to dam back than water. Just when we the students of HBTU think we have it impounded safe. The time has come when many more of us had to spur. We have to spur out of the dust and smoke from your firing soul.


Hearts are heavy

Souls are weary

Eyes are blurry

If you do not see

tears on the cheek

This know...:

Much crying - the eyes are weak.

So, E-Cell HBTU has come up with a city wide collection drive for people suffering in Kerala. The drive will include :

1.) The basic day to day things we don't even use.

2.) Clothes, bedsheets, rain coats, umbrella.
